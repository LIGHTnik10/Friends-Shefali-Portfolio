About me

Therapy that opens doors to a fuller life.

I'm an Occupational Therapist and Center Head with hands-on experience supporting adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities. My work blends skill development, vocational training, community participation and family engagement into pathways that lead to real independence.

Across clinical, school, home and community settings, I've grown comfortable with programme implementation, stakeholder coordination, admissions support, documentation and multidisciplinary collaboration. Above all, I'm driven by a belief that everyone deserves a meaningful, participating place in society.
